The mark

  • A lowercase “j” for Jot — a single confident pen-stroke, the way you’d flick down a note.
  • The tittle (the dot) doubles as a data point — a quiet nod to the insights and analytics coming later.
  • The stroke lifts slightly at the tail: a jotting motion, and a subtle upward line.
  • One idea, one letter — legible at thumbnail size and ownable as the product grows.
